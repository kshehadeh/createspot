Create Spot is a place where creators build portfolios, share work at the visibility they choose, and art lovers discover that work in exhibits and feeds.

![Create Spot homepage](/images/homepage.png)

## For creators

Build your profile, curate your portfolio, and share pieces with public, profile-only, or private visibility.

## For art lovers

Browse exhibits, follow creators, and explore the community feed and galleries.

## What you'll find in this help

- **Getting Started** — Quick paths for creators and art lovers.
- **Creators** — Profile, portfolio, submissions, and feedback.
- **Browsers** — Exhibits, views (grid and path), and discovery.
- **Inspiration** — Museums and collection browsing.
- **Administration** — For admins: users, exhibits, and notifications.

Use the sidebar to jump to any section.
