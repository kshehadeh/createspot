Create Spot is a place where creators share work inspired by weekly prompts and art lovers discover and explore that work.

## For creators

Build your profile, curate your portfolio, and share pieces inspired by three-word prompts each week.

## For art lovers

Browse exhibits, follow creators, and explore prompt-based galleries.

## What you'll find in this help

- **Getting Started** — Quick paths for creators and art lovers.
- **Creators** — Profile, portfolio, submissions, and feedback.
- **Browsers** — Exhibits, views (grid and map), and discovery.
- **Inspiration** — Prompts and prompt-based work.
- **Administration** — For admins: users, exhibits, prompts, and notifications.
