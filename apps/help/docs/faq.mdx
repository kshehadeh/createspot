## General

### What is Create Spot?

Create Spot is a community where creators respond to weekly three-word prompts with photos or text, and art lovers browse exhibits and discover work.

### Do I need an account to browse?

You can explore public exhibits and prompt galleries. An account is required to submit work, manage your profile, and use certain features.

## Creators

### How do I submit to a prompt?

Go to the current week's prompt (e.g. from Inspiration or Play), add your photo or text, and submit. See [Submitting your prompt work](/inspiration/prompts/submitting-prompt-work).

### Can I make my profile private?

Yes. See [Public vs. Private Profiles](/creators/profile/public-vs-private) for visibility options.

### How do I download my work?

See [Downloading your work](/creators/portfolio/downloading-work) for how to export or download your submissions.

## Browsers

### What's the difference between Grid and Map view?

[Exhibit views](/browsers/exhibits/exhibit-views-grid-path) explains Grid (layout of pieces) and Map (spatial or path-based) views for exhibits.

### Are exhibits permanent or temporary?

See [Permanent Exhibits vs. Temporary Ones](/browsers/exhibits/permanent-vs-temporary) for how exhibit dates and visibility work.

## Administration

### How do I get admin access?

Admin roles are managed by existing admins. See [Admin vs. User roles](/administration/users/admin-vs-user-roles).

### Where do I manage prompts?

Admins manage prompts in the admin area. See [Prompts](/administration/prompts) and [Creating New Prompts](/administration/prompts/creating-prompts).
