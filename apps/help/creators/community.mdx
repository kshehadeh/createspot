The **Community** page lets you see who follows you, who you follow, and manage blocked users. Open it from the menu (top right) → **My Hub** → **Community**.

![Community page](/images/community.png)

## What's on the Community page

- **Followers** — People who follow you. You can block anyone from this list.
- **Following** — Creators you follow. Unfollow anytime from this list.
- **Blocked** — People you've blocked. You can unblock them here.
