Your profile is how other users discover you and your work. You can control what appears and who can see it.

Open your **Profile** from the menu (top right), then choose **Edit profile** to update your info, space, and visibility.

![Profile editor](/images/profile-edit.png)

## What's in your profile

- **About you** — Information about yourself and how to find you on the web. See [Information about yourself and findability](/creators/profile/info-and-findability).
- **Your space** — Featured piece, protections (e.g. download, AI training), tutorials, and language. See [Setup your space](/creators/profile/setup-your-space).
- **Visibility** — Whether your profile is public or private. See [Public vs. Private Profiles](/creators/profile/public-vs-private).
- **Badges** — Achievements and recognition. See [Badges](/creators/profile/badges).
