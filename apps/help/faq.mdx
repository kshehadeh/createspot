## General

### What is Create Spot?

Create Spot is a community where creators build portfolios, share work with flexible visibility, and art lovers browse exhibits, feeds, and creator profiles.

### Do I need an account to browse?

You can explore public exhibits and much of the gallery without signing in. An account is required to add work, manage your profile, follow creators, and use certain features.

## Creators

### How do I add work?

Use **Portfolio** → **Edit portfolio** to create a new piece or move existing work onto your portfolio. See [Working with submissions](/creators/portfolio/working-with-submissions).

### Can I make my profile private?

Yes. See [Public vs. Private Profiles](/creators/profile/public-vs-private) for visibility options.

### How do I download my work?

See [Downloading your work](/creators/portfolio/downloading-work) for how to export or download your submissions.
